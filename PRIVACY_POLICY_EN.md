# Privacy Policy - FlixPlayer

**Effective Date:** January 21, 2026

## 1. General Information

FlixPlayer is a multimedia player application developed for Android devices. This privacy policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our application.

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Automatic Information
- **App usage data:** Information about how you use the application, including video playbacks and navigation
- **Device information:** Device model, operating system version, unique device identifiers
- **Performance data:** Information about app performance and technical errors

### 2.2 Third-Party Information
- **Google AdMob:** Collects data to display personalized ads
- **Start.io:** Collects data to display personalized ads
- **Firebase Analytics:** Collects usage data to improve the application

## 3. How We Use Your Information

### 3.1 Primary Purposes
- Provide and maintain the multimedia playback service
- Improve user experience
- Display relevant advertisements
- Analyze app usage for future improvements

### 3.2 Advertising
- We use Google AdMob and Start.io to display advertisements
- Ads may be personalized based on your interests
- You can opt out of personalized ads in your device settings

## 4. Information Sharing

### 4.1 Authorized Third Parties
We share limited information with:
- **Google AdMob:** To display advertisements
- **Start.io:** To display advertisements
- **Firebase:** For analytics and push notifications

### 4.2 We Don't Sell Data
We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties.

## 5. Data Storage

### 5.1 Local Data
- M3U8 playlists are stored locally on your device
- Configuration data is saved in local storage
- We do not store multimedia content on our servers

### 5.2 Data Retention
- Analytics data is retained according to Firebase policies
- Advertising data is retained according to AdMob and Start.io policies

## 6. Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

## 7. Your Rights

### 7.1 Ad Control
- You can disable personalized ads in your device's Google settings
- You can reset your advertising ID at any time

### 7.2 App Data
- You can delete all app data by uninstalling the application
- You can clear playlists from the app settings

## 8. Children

FlixPlayer is not directed at children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

## 9. Policy Changes

We may update this privacy policy occasionally. We will notify you of significant changes by posting the new policy on this page.
